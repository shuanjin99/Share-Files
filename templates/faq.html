FAQ

 

 

 

Accessing your account

Why has the login changed from username to email?

Social sign in is here! Our new identity management system is currently being rolled out which will offer a lot of exciting features for the Best Recipes audience in the future.

If you choose not to login via social media, you will need to use your email address and existing password.

You will then need to enter your first and last name to complete your profile within the 'registration' process. If you don't do this, your form will error.

Saving recipes

How do I save a recipe?

Every recipe on the site can be saved and organised into personal collections of folders in your own recipe book. To save just click the on the save button, located directly underneath the recipe description. You will be able to select an existing recipe book or save into the default My Favourites folder. You can access all of your saved items by clicking on the Edit Profile button at the top right corner of the page, and selecting My Recipe Book tab.

How do I create a new folder in my Recipe Book?

To create a new folder, access your My Recipe Book in your profile. Click on the New Folder button and give your new folder a name. At this stage you can't create a new folder from a recipe page. You can edit the folders name at any time by clicking on the edit button (looks like a pencil) and re-naming the folder.

How do I add one of my recipes?

You can add your original recipes on our Submit a Recipe page. We've made some updates to our recipe submission form since we relaunched in September 2014. We've heard your feedback that it was a bit tricky or time-consuming to share a recipe so we've simplified the process removing some mandatory fields and allowing you to add ingredients and measurements as text. You can now take a photo on your mobile phone or add it straight from your phone's camera roll.

Reviews

How do I make a comment or share a review of a recipe?

Only members are able to review and comment on recipes. The comment/review section is at the bottom of each recipe. You can leave a comment with what you thought of the recipe, any changes you made or would make next time and a star rating (1-5). We ask that our home cooks be polite and factual when sharing their opinions of recipes. Our editorial team reserve the right to remove or edit comments that breach our terms and conditions.

How do I make a comment or share a review of a recipe?

If you'd like to make some changes to a recipe or a review that you have submitted, you can send an email to team at contactus@iDiet.com.sg Please include your username, the recipe name, the URL of the recipe and the details of changes you would like made.

Site updates

Where has the menu planner gone to?

The menu planner was not moved over to the new Best Recipes. We've found that our members aren't using the menu planner in its current format but may re-invent this feature in future.

I really miss a feature from the old site - are you going to bring it back?

The Best Recipes community is so passionate about creating and sharing recipes. We're listening to your feedback and using this to help inform the building of new features and fixing bugs and issues with the site. If you have feedback please send the team an email at contactus@iDiet.com.sg.
